Under the hood

Seven free services, each with one job

📦GitHub
Code storage
Google Drive for code. Every change is version-controlled and reversible — I can rewind any edit.
FREE
🌐GitHub Pages
Website hosting
Serves the app to the world. Push a change, it goes live in about a minute.
FREE
🗄️Supabase
Database (filing cabinet)
The vault where check-ins, food logs, water, and notes actually live. Locked to my private token.
FREE
🛎️Render
API server (front-desk clerk)
The friendly middleman. When my phone saves something, Render files it in Supabase. When my laptop asks for the latest, Render fetches it. Keeps every device in sync.
FREE
🛡️Cloudflare Worker
Strava go-between
Holds the Strava secret in a safe place and hands my workouts back to the app. Nothing sensitive ever touches the public page.
FREE
🍎Open Food Facts
Nutrition database
A giant, community-run food database. When I search "cappuccino," this is where the macros come from — no account needed.
FREE
🏃Strava
Workout source
Where my Apple Watch workouts already sync automatically. My app just borrows them from here.
FREE

Real-world example

What happens when I log water on my phone

1
📱 Phone
I tap "+36" on the water card while walking to the kitchen.
2
🛎️ Render
The app quietly hands the update to my Render server — proving it's me with my private token.
3
🗄️ Supabase
Render files it in Supabase, which stores it forever.
4
💻 Laptop
When I open my laptop later, the app asks Render "what's new?" and my water shows up already.
