Under the Hood — seven free services, each with one job

📦GitHub
Code Storage
Google Drive for code. Every change is version-controlled and reversible.
🌐GitHub Pages
Website Hosting
Serves the app to the world. Push a change, it's live in a minute.
🗄️Supabase
Database
The vault. Holds all check-ins, food logs, water, notes — locked to my token.
🛎️Render
API Server
The middleman. Files data into Supabase, fetches it back. Keeps devices in sync.
🛡️Cloudflare Worker
Strava Go-Between
Holds the Strava secret safely and hands my workouts back to the app.
🍎Open Food Facts
Nutrition Database
A giant, community-run food database. Powers the "🌐 Food DB" search.
🏃Strava
Workout Source
Where Apple Watch workouts already sync. The app just borrows from here.
⌚Apple Health / Watch
The Original Sensor
Records the workout in the first place, feeding Strava automatically.

Real-World Example — logging water on my phone

1
📱 Phone
Tap "+36" on the water card while walking to the kitchen.
2
🛎️ Render
App hands the update to my Render server — my private token proves it's me.
3
🗄️ Supabase
Render files it in Supabase, which stores it forever.
4
💻 Laptop
Later, the laptop asks "what's new?" and the water shows up already.
